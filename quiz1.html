Introduction

This learning module will focus on the different parts of speech within the English language. There are eight grammatical parts of speech: nouns, pronouns, verbs, adjectives, prepositions, conjunctions, and interjections.

A part of speech is a category to which a word is assigned in a sentence by its syntactic meaning. Each part of speech explains not what the word is, but how it is used. In fact, many words can function as more than one part of speech. Understanding how words function in a sentence, paragraph, and an essay will improve your speaking and writing skills making you an effective communicator.
